## Common AC Problems in Mississauga Homes

Air conditioning systems can develop issues over time, especially during peak summer use. The common AC problems are as follows:

- AC not turning on due to electrical issues, thermostat problems, or component failure
- Warm air from vents which is usually linked to refrigerant issues, compressor problems, or airflow restrictions
- Weak airflow caused by dirty filters, blocked vents, or other duct issues
- Grinding or buzzing noises which indicate mechanical issues
- Strange odors which can signal electrical or mold-related problems
- Clogged drains or frozen evaporator coils that can lead to leaks

## Common Furnace Problems in Mississauga Homes

Furnace issues become more noticeable during colder months when consistent heating is critical. Homeowners in the area often complain about the following issues:

- No heat or insufficient heating due to ignition problems, thermostat issues, or airflow restrictions
- Some rooms may feel colder due to duct issues or system imbalance
- Frequent cycling or short cycling indicating thermostat issues or system inefficiency
- Rattling or banging sounds often pointing to loose components or internal wear
- Higher energy bills due to an inefficient furnace that tends to consume more energy
